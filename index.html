Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?

All payments are processed securely and quickly through M-PESA, directly to your registered phone number.

Is this program officially run by SEKU?

This website is an. initiative to connect SEKU students with opportunities. The earning platform itself, TrendQash Agencies, is a third-party service provider.

What is the one-time activation fee?

TrendQash Agencies requires a one-time activation fee of **Kes550** to unlock lifetime access to all earning features. This fee helps maintain the platform and serves as a security measure to avoid non-serious students from registering many accounts.

Do I need any special skills?

Absolutely not! The tasks are designed to be simple and require no prior experience. All you need is a smartphone and an internet connection.
